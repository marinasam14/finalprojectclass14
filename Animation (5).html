Workers at Risk in NYC: Exposing Deadly Injuries at Companies

by Nicol Leon and Marina Samuel. Updated May 19th 2025

This search tool, based on an OSHA database, provides information about companies located in New York City where workers have suffered serious physical injuries resulting in hospitalizations, amputations, or the loss of an eye. The goal is to help users identify companies they may be considering for employment that have a history of such incidents, through a search by ZIP code or industry. The tool provides details including the date of the incident, the company involved, and a description of the injury sustained by the worker.



This database, called "Severe Injury Reports," was collected by OSHA under Law 29-CFR 1904.39, which requires businesses to report these occurrences in their workplaces.
